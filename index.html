How far in advance should we book?+
We recommend 12–18 months for the best venue availability and pricing, especially in peak season (December–April). Shorter timelines can work depending on guest count and venue.
Do you handle Indian catering?+
Yes — specialist caterers offer authentic vegetarian, Jain, halal and regional menus for every event, from Mehndi breakfast to the Reception dinner.
Can you arrange a Pandit and mandap?+
Absolutely. We coordinate experienced priests, custom mandap design, havan kund and all ritual materials with full cultural authenticity.
What about travel from the US and Canada?+
We manage group airfare with negotiated rates. Punta Cana has 3–4 hour direct flights, and no visa is required for US, Canadian or Mexican nationals.
How many events can we host?+
Most couples host 3 to 5 events across the multi-day itinerary — welcome dinner, Mehndi/Haldi, Sangeet, the ceremony and the Reception.
Is a DR wedding legally recognised?+
Yes. We provide complete legal guidance and assist with all documentation and permits so your wedding is officially recognised.
